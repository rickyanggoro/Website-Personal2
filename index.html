Graphic Desain

Graphic design is the art and practice of planning and projecting ideas and experiences with visual and textual content. It involves creative and systematic approaches to solve problems or achieve specific objectives, using typography, photography, and illustration. Graphic designers utilize various tools and techniques to create visual compositions that communicate messages effectively, whether for branding, advertising, publications, or digital media. Effective graphic design not only enhances aesthetics but also aims to convey information clearly and attractively to its intended audience, thereby playing a crucial role in shaping perceptions and influencing decisions.

Web Development

Web development refers to the process of building and maintaining websites and web applications. It encompasses a wide range of tasks, including web design, front-end development, back-end development, and server management. Web developers use programming languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create the structure, layout, and interactivity of web pages.
